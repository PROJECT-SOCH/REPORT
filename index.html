Project SOCH

Seeds of Change & Hope

1. Introduction

Project SOCH is a community-led initiative aimed at empowering students and individuals from diverse backgrounds through awareness, education, and engagement. It provides platforms for open discussion, growth, and guidance on important issues affecting personal and academic development.

2. Objectives

  • To promote career awareness among school students.
  • To deliver adult education on essential life skills.
  • To build a supportive community environment for learning and self-expression.

3. Target Groups

School students, children, and youth from diverse communities, as well as adults needing foundational education on topics like financial literacy, digital skills, and personal well-being.

4. Key Components of Project SOCH

  • Career Awareness & Guidance – Sessions to help students understand career options and pathways.
  • Adult Education – Topics include financial literacy, digital skills, legal rights, etc.
  • Interactive Community Sessions – Activities around good/bad touch, periods, bullying, puberty, and personality development.

5. Session Plans

Topics Covered:
  • Good Touch, Bad Touch
  • Bullying and Peer Pressure
  • Career Guidance
  • Periods and Male Puberty
  • Personality Development

6. Implementation Strategy

Sessions are planned in collaboration with schools and community centers. Trained volunteers lead interactive activities and discussions in safe, inclusive environments.

7. School Activities & Reports

  • Session Documentation Format: Includes name, date, topic, facilitators, and feedback.
  • Activity Reports: Summary of events and participation metrics.
  • Case Studies: Real-life impact stories shared with consent.

8. Monitoring & Evaluation

Feedback forms, participant surveys, and session reviews are used to measure engagement, learning, and outcomes. Continuous improvements are made based on feedback.

9. Expected Outcomes

  • Increased awareness on social and personal topics
  • More confident and informed students and adults
  • Empowered communities with better decision-making skills

10. Future Scope & Expansion

Project SOCH aims to partner with more schools, NGOs, and community spaces across India, train more volunteers, and digitize content for broader reach.
